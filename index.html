Program Purpose

Translate Japanese urban transport practice into options for Greater Cairo.

The program combines lectures, site visits, discussions, and project workshops. Its focus is integrated land-use and transport planning, metropolitan policy, transit-oriented development, corridor development, smart-city operations, financing approaches, and follow-up action planning for the Cairo transport strategy project.

Expected Outputs

  • Shared understanding of Japan's metropolitan planning and transport policy tools.
  • Practical lessons from Tokyo, Yokohama, Tokyu corridor, Tsukuba Express, and Kashiwa-no-ha.
  • Project discussion notes with PCKK and JICA teams.
  • Three team presentations summarizing lessons and application ideas.
